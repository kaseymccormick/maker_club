Exporting and slicing

Kasey McCormick | March 62018

export your design into an .stl

After you've exported your design into an .stl do the followint

  • Go to astroprint.com
  • Click log in
  • Enter username/email mccormick.kasey@gmail.com
  • Enter password MakerClub2018
  • Click design uploader
  • a file browser window shows up, find your exported tinkercad file (.stl)
  • click open
  • 1. choose your printer should already be set to i3 mk2
  • 2. choose your material should already be set to PLA
  • 3. Print quality should be set to maker club setting
  • click slice
  • you’ll be brought to a new window, click on the i in a circle on the far right on the same line as your object
  • it’ll scroll you down to your print file
  • click the plus sign under your picture, this is where you’ll find the data to put on your worksheet.
